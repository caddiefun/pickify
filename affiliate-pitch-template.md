## Tips for Higher Approval Rates

### Do:
- ✅ Apply with a professional email (contact@pickify.io, not gmail)
- ✅ Have your site live with real content before applying
- ✅ Include specific pages where you'll feature the product
- ✅ Mention your traffic sources (organic = good)
- ✅ Be honest about current traffic—even small sites get approved
- ✅ Follow up after 5-7 days if no response

### Don't:
- ❌ Apply to programs in categories you don't cover
- ❌ Inflate traffic numbers (they may verify)
- ❌ Use generic copy-paste applications
- ❌ Apply multiple times to the same program
- ❌ Mention competitors negatively

## Network-Specific Notes

### Impact (impact.com)
- Largest network for tech/SaaS affiliates
- Apply to the network first, then individual programs
- Some programs auto-approve, others take 1-7 days
- Products: NordVPN, ExpressVPN, Norton, 1Password

### Commission Junction (cj.com)
- Established network with strict quality standards
- May require phone verification
- Products: Bitdefender, McAfee, GoDaddy, AT&T

### ShareASale
- Good for hosting and smaller SaaS products
- Quick approval process
- Products: Bluehost, WP Engine, many hosting providers

### Partnerstack
- B2B SaaS focused
- Often higher commissions (recurring)
- Products: Monday.com, Notion, HubSpot

### Direct/In-House Programs
- Usually found in website footer ("Affiliates" or "Partners")
- Often best commission rates
- More personal relationship with affiliate managers
- Examples: Surfshark, SiteGround, Proton
